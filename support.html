Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Pro subscription?

Go to Settings → Subscription → "Restore purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

A word or translation looks wrong — how do I report it?

Open the word card, scroll down, and tap "Report a Problem". Select the issue type and optionally add a comment. We review all reports and fix errors in updates.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. The entire dictionary — all 11,000+ words, conjugations, examples and flashcards — works completely offline. No internet connection or account is required.

What's included in AlefBet Pro?

Pro unlocks the full dictionary across all CEFR levels, unlimited favorites (Free is limited to 20), and flashcards for every level. The Free version covers the A1–A2 core.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → AlefBet → Cancel Subscription. You can also reach this from Settings → Subscription inside the app.

What languages are supported?

Every word has Russian and English translations and transcription. The interface language can be set to Russian or English in Settings.
