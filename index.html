Features

Everything you need
to read and learn Hebrew

From beginner A1 to advanced C2 — a clean, fast dictionary that works entirely on your device.

11,000+ Words

A comprehensive dictionary with nikud (vowel marks), modern spelling, and CEFR levels from A1 to C2.

Root & Binyan

Every verb shows its Hebrew root (shoresh) and binyan, with past, present and future forms. Understand word families and patterns.

Russian & English

Each word comes with a clear translation and transcription in both Russian and English, so the pronunciation is never a guess.

Flashcards & Quizzes

Turn any level or letter into a deck. Swipe to learn, test yourself, and review the words you didn't know.

Favorites & Recents

Save words to favorites and revisit everything you've looked up. Build your own study list as you go.

Works Offline

The entire dictionary lives on your device. Search, browse and study anywhere — no internet, no account, no tracking.
